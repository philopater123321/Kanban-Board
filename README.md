# Kanban Board
Kanban board is a kind of productive people apps, it is about you can put your tasks in three parts, which are to do section, in progress section, and finally the Done section.
This program is really good, and it is not like other to do list apps, as it is more attractive, and more better, and has many features, and lets you know that status of your task.

<img width="1918" height="907" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da7e7706-34c9-4693-8f1d-2b815de1607a" />


### Features:
- **Local Storage:** any task you add is locally storaged and when you refresh the program, the tasks don't disappear, and also if you quit the program, nothing happens.
- **Changing the name of the task**: After you add your task and if you want to rename it you can just double click on it and rename it as you want.
  <img width="1918" height="920" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/32cade02-a998-4d73-b625-c9b75ec1439e" />
- **Dragging and dropping:** You can drag and drop any task you want to any part of the three sections
  <img width="1913" height="891" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/483edbd0-465f-4b2c-97b6-96cd65c09315" />
- **Clear all, in done part:** In Done part after you finish all of your tasks you can clear them all to start from scratch again.
  <img width="1918" height="923" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cbf8bc7c-84c9-43e3-afc9-94fac23d7e4f" />
- **Counting System:** Each section have a counter for the tasks.
  <img width="1917" height="908" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad8564c7-e484-448f-89b5-d1b25a2e7ec5" />
- **Priority colors:** Each priority level gives the tasks a color like, the low level priority has green, the medium has orange, and the high has red color.
<img width="1917" height="911" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7553b91d-8e5f-45b3-8ec5-5d7a83f85b1a" />
- **the delete Button (X):** When you press X you can delete that task
- **Dark Mode:** You can open dark mode in your program if you preffer it.
